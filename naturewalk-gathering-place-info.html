NATUREWALK GATHERING PLACE INFORMATION

This page includes key stay guidelines and departure instructions to help your trip go smoothly.

Bike & Bike Gear

  • Bikes are provided with the unit. Please secure the bikes in the designated storage area if you are not using them
  • To reserve additional bikes and bike gear, please contact with Dream Destinations 30A.

House Rules

No Smoking
Smoking is not permitted within the home.
No Pets
Prior written approval is required for any pets.
Air Conditioner Settings
Maintain the air conditioner above 70°F for optimal use.
Linen and Towels
All provided linen and towels are for in-home use only, excluding beach towels.
Wet Towels
Refrain from hanging wet towels on furniture or railings.
Furniture and Accessories
Return all furniture and accessories to their original locations before departure.
Exterior Doors
Keep exterior doors closed.
Community Rules and Noise Ordinances
Adhere to all community rules and local noise ordinances. Any complaints may result in management involvement, law enforcement referral, and potential eviction.
Occupancy Policies
Strictly adhere to occupancy policies and limits. These guidelines are in place for your safety and the protection of the vacation home and rental industry. Violation may lead to immediate eviction with complete forfeiture of all payments.
Additional Guests
Obtain prior approval for any guests beyond the specified limit.
Your cooperation ensures a harmonious stay and is greatly appreciated. Thank you for being respectful to the home, neighbors, and the community.

Departure Guidelines

Checkout Time
  • The standard checkout time is 10:00 AM.
  • Late checkouts will result in a $200.00 fee (covers housekeeping and scheduling).
  • If you plan to leave before 10:00 AM, please inform us through the designated App.
Damage Reporting
Report any accidental damage before departure to be covered by our Accidental Damage policy.
Trash & Dishes
  • Ensure all trash is taken out before departure.
  • Wash all dishes or run them through the dishwasher.
Linens
All linens are professionally laundered upon departure. Please refrain from washing linens or terry.
A/C Setting
Set the A/C temperature to cool 75°F.
Door Lock
Confirm that the door lock is set to lock the door securely.
Wristbands / Passes (If Applicable)
Leave all wristbands or passes on the kitchen counter by the coffee maker.
Pool Cards (If Applicable)
Please leave the pool card on the kitchen counter by the coffee maker.
Golf Cart (If Applicable)
Plug in and cover the golf cart before departure.
We are a full-service rental company and want to make sure you are as happy as possible with your vacation. If you experience any issues at all during your stay, please call us.
