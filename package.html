PACKAGE DELIVERY

Accepted Deliveries

  • Packages can only be delivered to the front desk during office hours.
  • Guests must sign for all deliveries.

Delivery Hours

  • Deliveries are accepted between 9:00 AM – 5:00 PM, Monday–Saturday.
  • No deliveries are accepted on Sundays or holidays.

Package Pickup

  • Guests must present ID or confirmation to pick up packages.
  • Please collect packages within 24 hours of arrival.

Restrictions

  • Oversized or heavy items may not be accepted.
  • Perishable goods are not guaranteed to be stored properly.
