Things To Do

THINGS TO DO NEARBY

Scenic Rides & Nature Trails

  • Rent bikes and explore the 30A bike path, coastal dune lakes, and neighborhood trails.
  • Visit nearby state parks for walking trails, birdwatching, and quiet beach access.
  • Sunset rides are especially beautiful along the coastal path.

Family-Friendly Activities

  • Visit local town centers for splash pads, playgrounds, and live music events (seasonal).
  • Mini-golf, arcades, and ice cream stops are all within a short drive.
  • Look for outdoor movie nights and seasonal festivals in the area.

Transportation

  • WaterColor Trolley Shuttle Service : A free service for homeowners and rental guests, operating in-season (April - Labor Day) from 9:30 AM – 5:30 PM. The trolley circulates through neighborhoods, pools, Camp WaterColor, and the Beach Club.
  • Golf Cart Rental: LSVs can only be rented through Electric Cart Company (850-622-2000). Rentals are limited to 80 LSVs on the property, so book early, especially during holidays. Unauthorized LSVs are subject to towing. (WaterColor properties accept golf cart rentals only throught Electric Cart Company)

On the Water

  • Book kayak or paddleboard rentals to explore coastal dune lakes or the Gulf (weather permitting).
  • Consider dolphin cruises, fishing charters, or sunset boat tours from nearby marinas.
  • Always follow local safety instructions and life jacket requirements.
