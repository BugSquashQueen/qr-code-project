THINGS TO DO NEARBY

Scenic Rides & Nature Trails

  • Rent bikes and explore the 30A bike path, coastal dune lakes, and neighborhood trails.
  • Visit nearby state parks for walking trails, birdwatching, and quiet beach access.
  • Sunset rides are especially beautiful along the coastal path.

Family-Friendly Activities

  • Visit local town centers for splash pads, playgrounds, and seasonal live music.
  • Seaside events calendar: Click here
  • Mini-golf, arcades, and ice cream stops are all within a short drive.
  • Look for outdoor movie nights and seasonal festivals in the area.

Transportation

  • WaterColor Trolley Shuttle Service: Click here
  • Please contact our office for multiple golf cart rentals.
  • For WaterColor: second golf cart rentals are accepted only through Electric Cart Company (850-622-2000).
