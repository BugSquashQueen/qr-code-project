House Rules

HOUSE RULES

No Smoking

  • Smoking is not allowed inside the property.

No Pets

  • If you are not staying in one of our designated pet-friendly properties, pets are strictly prohibited. Our pet policy is very firm, and any exceptions require prior written approval.

Quiet Hours

  • Please respect quiet hours from 10:00 PM – 8:00 AM.

Guest Policy

  • Strictly adhere to occupancy policies and limits. These guidelines are in place for your safety and the protection of the vacation home and rental industry. Violation may lead to immediate eviction with complete forfeiture of all payments.
  • Additional Guests: Obtain prior approval for any guests beyond the specified limit.

Respect Property

  • No parties or large gatherings.
  • Do not move furniture without approval.
  • Air Conditioner Settings: Maintain the air conditioner 74 degrees for optimal use.
  • Linen and Towels: All provided linen and towels are for in-home use only, excluding beach towels.
  • Wet Towels: Refrain from hanging wet towels on furniture or railings.
  • Furniture and Accessories: Return all furniture and accessories to their original locations before departure.
  • Exterior Doors: Keep exterior doors closed.
  • Trash: Trash pickup is on Mondays and Thursdays. Note the strict HOA regulations. Kindly ensure that the bear straps are properly fastened to the trash can to maintain a secure and wildlife-resistant environment.
