  • Ed Walline Public Beach Access: 4447 W County Highway 30A
  • Blue Mountain Regional Beach Access: 2365 S Scenic Hwy 83
  • For more public beach access options, please check Walton County Interactive Map link.

For WaterColor Guests

  • Available for registered WaterColor guests only.
  • Wristbands must be visible upon entry and during your visit.
  • Camp WaterColor: 205 Spartina Circle

What is a regional beach access?

  • Regional Beach Access (RBA) offers public beach access via boardwalk with restrooms, parking, showers, bike racks as well as ADA access in select locations.
