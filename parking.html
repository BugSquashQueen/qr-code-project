Parking Rules

PARKING RULES

Quick parking reminder for NatureWalk guests: Please park only in designated parking areas. Parking on streets, sidewalks (including tires on the sidewalk), landscaped areas, district-owned roadways, or with tires outside marked spaces is not allowed and is a tow-away zone. Any towing will be at the guest’s expense. Thank you for your cooperation.

General Rules

  • Park only in designated spaces for your unit or side parking areas.
  • Please check your check-in instructions for available parking spots on the property driveway.
  • No blocking of driveways, fire lanes, or other vehicles.
  • Always display parking permits if provided.

Restricted Areas

  • No parking on grass, sidewalks, or landscaped areas.
  • Golf carts must be parked in the driveway or garage only. Street parking is prohibited. Carts parked on the street will be towed at the guest’s expense.
  • Loading/unloading zones must remain clear except for active use.
  • Parking Violation: If unauthorized parking results in a ticket, you will be responsible for the fee, which will be added to your reservation and charged to the card on file.
  • When not in use, please park the golf cart in the provided garage, if the garage is available for guest use.
  • Please avoid parking golf carts on any grass areas. The Sheriff’s Office issues fines for parking on grass. We will charge your credit card for any violations.
  • Ensure the golf cart is fully charged before removing it from the property.
  • You may charge the cart during times it is not in use for your convenience.
  • Handicap spaces are reserved strictly for vehicles with valid placards.

Vehicle Requirements

  • All vehicles must be operable, licensed, and insured.
  • No overnight parking of trailers, boats, or oversized vehicles unless approved.
  • Report accidents, damage, or violations immediately.

WATERCOLOR PARKING

For WaterColor properties: Please read your check-in instructions carefully for parking pass information.

Important Information for WaterColor Properties

  • If you park in any designated side parking areas within WaterColor, ensure your parking pass is visibly displayed in your vehicle.
  • If you park in the property’s driveway, you do not need to display the parking pass, as long as the vehicle is parked in the driveway.
