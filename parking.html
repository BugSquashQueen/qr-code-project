PARKING RULES

NatureWalk reminder: Park only in designated areas. Parking on streets, sidewalks (including tires on the sidewalk), landscaped areas, district-owned roadways, or with tires outside marked spaces is not allowed and is a tow-away zone. Any towing is at the guest’s expense.

General Rules

  • Park only in designated spaces for your unit or side parking areas.
  • Please review your check-in instructions for driveway parking availability.
  • No blocking driveways, fire lanes, or other vehicles.
  • Display parking permits if provided.

Restricted Areas

  • No parking on grass, sidewalks, or landscaped areas.
  • Golf carts must be parked in the driveway or garage only. Street parking is prohibited (tow at guest expense).
  • Loading/unloading zones must remain clear except for active use.
  • If unauthorized parking results in a ticket, the fee will be added to your reservation and charged to the card on file.
  • When not in use, park the golf cart in the garage if available for guest use.
  • Avoid parking golf carts on grass. Sheriff’s Office may issue fines (charged to card on file).
  • Ensure the golf cart is fully charged before removing it from the property.
  • You may charge the cart during times it is not in use.
  • Handicap spaces are reserved for vehicles with valid placards.

Vehicle Requirements

  • All vehicles must be operable, licensed, and insured.
  • No overnight parking of trailers, boats, or oversized vehicles unless approved.
  • Report accidents, damage, or violations immediately.

WATERCOLOR PARKING

WaterColor: Please read your check-in instructions carefully for parking pass information.

Parking Pass Guidelines

  • If you park in designated side parking areas, display your parking pass in the vehicle.
  • If you park in the property driveway, a pass is not required as long as the vehicle is parked in the driveway.
