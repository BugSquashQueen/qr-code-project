GOLF CART RULES

Use & Eligibility

  • Driver must be 21+ with a valid driver’s license.
  • Seatbelts required where equipped; max passengers = number of seats.
  • No driving under the influence of alcohol or drugs.
  • Observe posted speed limits and all traffic laws.

Parking

  • Park in the driveway or garage only. No street parking (tow at guest expense).
  • Avoid parking on landscaping, grassy areas, sidewalks, nature trails, or boardwalks.
  • If unauthorized parking results in a ticket, the fee will be added to your reservation and charged to the card on file.

Charging & Fees

  • Ensure the golf cart is fully charged before removing it from the property.
  • Do not operate below 20% battery (charge monitored by management).
Fee: If operated under 20% and towing is required, a $150 fee will be charged to the card on file.

Keys

  • Golf cart keys are on the kitchen countertop; leave them there at checkout.
Missing keys: $100 fee charged to the card on file.
