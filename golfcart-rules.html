Golf Cart Rules

USE & ELIGIBILITY

  • Driver must be 25+ with a valid driver’s license.
  • Seatbelts required where equipped; max passengers = number of seats.
  • No driving under the influence of alcohol or drugs.

WHERE YOU MAY DRIVE

  • Community roads only where carts are permitted; no highways.
  • Observe posted speed limits and local ordinances.
  • Park only in designated areas; never on sidewalks or grass.

CARE & CHARGING

  • Charge overnight at the assigned outlet; return cord to holder.
  • Do not drive through standing water or on the beach.
  • Report damage immediately; renter is responsible for violations/fees.
